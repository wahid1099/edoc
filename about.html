About DocNow BD

Revolutionizing Healthcare Access in Bangladesh

Our Story

Founded in 2022, eDoc emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality healthcare accessible to every Bangladeshi citizen. What started as a small team's passion project has grown into Bangladesh's leading digital healthcare platform.

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare accessibility in Bangladesh by connecting patients with qualified healthcare providers through innovative digital solutions, ensuring timely and quality medical care for all.

Our Network

We have established partnerships with leading hospitals and clinics across major cities including Dhaka, Chattogram, and Rajshahi. Our network continues to grow to serve more communities.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we've helped thousands of patients connect with healthcare providers, reducing waiting times by 60% and improving the overall healthcare experience.

Whom We Serve

Doctors

Qualified medical professionals looking to expand their practice

Hospitals

Healthcare facilities seeking to streamline patient management

Patients

Individuals seeking convenient access to quality healthcare
